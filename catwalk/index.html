CATWALK

have you heard about catwalk yet?

it's this super cool discord bot that basically turns it into this big group chat


With Catwalk, you can:


send text

send images

send stickers

Get it on your server!

How to use it:

Having Manage Guild permissions, go into any channel on your server and do:


Catwalk, here | establishes that channel as the home channel

Catwalk, create [room] | creates a group chat for your server

Catwalk, join [room] | joins an existing group chat if it exists

Catwalk! | It says hi back!


alternatively,

You can skip those 3 steps by just doing

Catwalk, setup [room]

And Catwalk will do everything for you :)
