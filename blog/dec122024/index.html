12-20-2024: Stockings

This is the first time my family has ever done this, so it's still pretty alien to me... but they've put up these stockings with everyone's initials on them, and now every morning I wake up to a stocking full of chocolate, candy, pepsi cans or something along those lines. And they won't say who it was either. It's all the elf's doing. For one, I think it's very cute. Adorable. Charming even. I think this is the most "christmas" thing we get to do after the santa gifts and all. We haven't been blasting too much christmas music or done as many family reunions as in previous years because everyone now lives in an apartment complex 10 or 20 miles away from each other. On the other hand, it makes me feel a little bad when I wake up to 2 or 3 gifts and I don't get to give anything back. I mean, I can walk to the store and get some chocolates, but they're not exactly in my path. Still, I try to. I leave the comfort of my room, if you may, and make the trek to a store that sells hersheys or something then sneak back to put them in everyone else's stockings. Mom can't have too much candy, but I still get her sweets. I don't know what else to put there, it just makes sense.
