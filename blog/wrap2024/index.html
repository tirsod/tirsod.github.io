That's a wrap: 2024.

Well, that's that. A successful year full of achievements.

(Yes, the song I've been listening to all year was, in fact, my most played song.)

Now, don't get scared here, alright? Stay with me.

2023's closing act had me losing my job, and one of my most prized possessions to a text-and-driver. What I thought would be a 2-month recovery from that mess turned into a whole year's worth of paperwork and pain.

I've tried meditating on it, but no place in the world is silent enough for the amount of internalization I have yet to do.

It was very frustrating. And it still is.

But the world doesn't really stop for you. It never really slows down. It just keeps on spinning.

And for the longest time, while the world did its thing. There I was. On my own. In the dark.

They call it rock bottom, right. The lowest point of your life. Up until that point, at least.

Thinking about it now, I can hear the sea. The deep hum of the water. The sands shifting beneath my weight. The fish swimming beyond my grasp.

It sounds relaxing, right? It kind of is. Feels like it shouldn't be. Because that wasn't how it felt at the time.

But you wanna know something fun? Hitting rock bottom taught me a lot of things.

While I've become more anxious, I've also become more serene. And while I don't always have the patience of an angel, I'm also getting better every day.

Because I've learned how much more connecting with people can add to your life. And because I've seen how unexpectedly, how quickly everything can change.

And I am now grateful for those boring, normal days, when I get to enjoy life, as I know it, again...

So, you could say it's been better. I think I've been better.

About those achievements,

I'm still here. That's a pretty good one as it is. You are, too. Pat yourself on the back!

80-something streams, 20-ish songs, 50 drawings, does any of it matter?

Sort of,

The numbers alone really don't, but the proof that I exist is present in those works, and the insatiable thirst for knowledge I've rediscovered is like nothing I've ever felt before.

Every time I sit down to hone my skills, I feel closer to the skies, where my imagination freely flies, and the clouds are empty music sheets for my lullabies.

The world has become my canvas, and my greatest achievement will be making use of it all.

Next up, success:

A couple hundred dollars, a thousand transactions. Employee of the month, straight As for days. No, but really. Does any of that really matter?

It's not the mark of recognition that fills me with joy. That's nothing but a sheet of paper with a marking on the corner.

Is that how you'd measure success? No,

It's knowing that I've accomplished my goals by putting everything I had into it, It's understanding that I am my own masterpiece, The culmination of all my hard work.

It's making it to the end of the day with a new story to tell. It's seeing a loved one sport a smile on their face, More so knowing you're the cause of their joy.

...

Yeah? No. No, I am not okay. Well, actually, I am. Thanks for asking. You see,

I am here seeing that I'm really everything that I've got. I am here knowing that I'm the architect of my own life. I am here realizing that this year will only be as good as I make it out to be.

And I don't want to walk into the new year accepting defeat,

Thinking that I'm destined to fail until a miracle strikes. That someone else will come here to save me. That simply speaking my resolutions is enough. That by telling others I'll somehow secure myself some good karma.

And I don't want that to be you, either.

Stop giving up. Stop doing that. Stop putting yourself down. Stop undermining yourself.

You're not just "a guy". You're not an idiot. You're not a waste of time.

Come on, Put some heart into it.

You're so much more than that. It's almost impossible to put into words.

You're all that you've got.

Believe in yourself.

Start believing in yourself.

You got this. You can do this. You've done this before. You can do so much more! You can achieve so much! You can be a force of change in the world! You're a being of thought! That can create so much!

You're capable. You're truly amazing.

Tell yourself that.

And everything is gonna be fine.

...

I'm sure there's an even deeper pit for me to fall into in the future. After all, I'm still young.

Am I ready for whatever life is gonna throw at me?

I'm not sure.

But I'm not gonna let that stop me. I'm not gonna let fear stop me.

And when I get there, I'm gonna claw my way out once more. And I'll come out the other end much stronger.

And I'm gonna be ready for when it happens.

And when you're done reading this, I don't want you to come find me and say, "I'm so sorry this happened to you" and "I hope your next year is better,"

I want to hear you say, "I'm going to do my best." "I'm going to believe in myself." "I won't let fear stop me." "I can do it!!!"

No, I don't expect it to be easy. No, You don't have to be perfect. No, I won't shame you if you "failed". And no, You don't have to meet any expectations.

So long as you try, And you give it your best.

You'll be okay!

and yes, I'll be okay, I'm giving it my best!

That's all. From a human being to another human being,

Please,

Have yourself a happy new year.
