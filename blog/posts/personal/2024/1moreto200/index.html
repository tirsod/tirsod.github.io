1 more to 200

Or a couple hundred.

At the time of writing this, I sit at 199 followers on my Twitch channel. Just one follower shy of 200.

If you're reading this, it means we reached 200.

Thank you,

I realize looking at numbers alone and talking about them feels kind of shallow, but this number specifically means a little something more. To me, at least.

Every follower is another person who chose to trust my work. All that I do to keep this stream alive. The models, the sounds, the characters, the funny redeems… and asked to be here for when it all comes together. For whenever I hit the big scary button and it all goes live.

Thank you.

I'm not perfect. To this day I still miss chat messages. Sometimes I read them but I shy away from reading it out loud. Or I get distracted and I forget to act on them. Or I don't know how to respond and I say something that comes across a little dry.

But I do seriously appreciate everyone who passes by and decides to wave hi to me or everyone else in chat. It's a small gesture but it goes a long way. Don't be afraid to tell me how your day went! I like knowing what's going on all over the world.

I hope my streams can continue being a source of inspiration, joy, laughter, focus, thought, comfort, peace or maybe just the occasional break from all the things going on in life.

Thank you!

And please! Don't stop creating. You all make some wonderful stuff all the time. It is truly inspiring to me and to others.

You’re awesome.

Keep it up.

(Quite the christmas gift honestly!)
