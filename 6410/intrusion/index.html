CODENAME: Intrusion Protocol

DETAIL: Guidelines to follow to avoid further anomalies and ensure a safer environment.

SUMMARY:

Dear Citizen,

This document has been issued to you because you are returning or being returned to an area that has experienced a Special Distortion Event.

Whereas Abyss Events destroy entire areas and leave massive traces of corruption, the traces left behind by Distortion Events are minimal and, in the vast majority of cases, infrastructure is recuperable through reconstruction.

This doesn't mean, however, that these areas can be left unmonitored. Even if Distortion Events are usually followed by low or null weight manipulation, the entity still resides within the area and *can* become active once more.

To ensure the citizens safety, the project employs connects via protocols that allow for preventive measures to be performed, ensuring the entity remains DORMANT.

(In cases where dormancy is impossible, a PASSIVE state can be enforced.)

Intrusion Protocol is one such preventive measure. The Intrusion Protocol is a voluntary, 3-step procedure that creates a window for a temporary intrusion, allowing researchers to temporarily faux-legitimize the entity’s weights in the world.

PROTOCOL STATUS: PASSIVE

Dormancy in your reality is impossible.

The entity *might* talk to you.

If this happens, do not perform the protocol in front of it.

PROTOCOL PROCEDURE:

To be performed every week: NOW

  • Using your hands, count to 6. Count to 4. Then count to 10.
  • Inscribe the pattern of the eye on any piece of media. Instructions on how to draw the eye are attached to the back of this file.
  • Cover your eyes with your hands, close your eyes, then open them again. You can now stop covering your eyes.

A researcher will come to pick up the eye. You can ignore this notice after they do.

Stick to the noise, and remain static.
